Jacob Oakley | Human Resources Sergeant | United States Army Reserves

Military

I entered Basic Combat Training for the United States Army Reserves in April of 2008. After completing basic I went to Ft. Jackson and became proficient as a Human Resources Specialist. I then spent six years in the HHD for 1st BDE 102nd DIV. At that time I had been promoted out of the unit and spent a few months with the 2nd MSG before transfering to the 1186th DDSB.
